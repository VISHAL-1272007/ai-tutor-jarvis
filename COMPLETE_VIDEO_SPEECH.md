## üí° DELIVERY TIPS

**Pacing:**
- Speak at **120-150 words per minute** (not too fast, not too slow)
- Pause for **2 seconds** after showing something impressive
- Pause for **1 second** after each major feature

**Emphasis:**
- **BOLD words** in script = say them louder/stronger
- Numbers (30,000 students, 95 requests/min) = pause before AND after

**Energy Levels:**
- Opening: **HIGH energy** - grab attention
- Middle: **Steady, confident** - demonstrate features
- Technical: **Calm, professional** - build credibility
- Closing: **HIGH energy** - leave strong impression

**Body Language:**
- **Point** to important features on screen
- **Gesture** when saying "imagine" or "think about"
- **Smile** when showing impressive features
- **Look at camera** during opening and closing

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
- ‚ùå Don't say "um", "uh", "like"
- ‚ùå Don't rush through demonstrations
- ‚ùå Don't read the script robotically
- ‚ùå Don't look down at notes too much
- ‚ùå Don't minimize your achievement

**Power Moves:**
- ‚úÖ Show LIVE generation (not pre-made screenshots)
- ‚úÖ Mention "30,000 students" early and confidently
- ‚úÖ Demonstrate on BOTH laptop and mobile
- ‚úÖ Have a backup demo ready in case of internet issues
- ‚úÖ End with strong eye contact and smile
